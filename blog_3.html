Here is my offical list of my favorite YouTube channels to help you learn guitar:

  1. Marin Music Center: This is by far my favorite. Very easy to follow and very funny.
  2. Guitar Goddess: Really good for quick easy tutorials if you want to sing to your favorite popular songs.
  3. Marty Music: Great if you are a beginner learning chords for the first. It starts off easy and gets incrementally harder so it doesn't get overwhelming. The tutorials can be a bit repetitive if you aren't new, so keep that in mind.
