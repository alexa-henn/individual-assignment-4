The Hardest and Easiest Chords to Learn

I hate bar chords.

Chords My Opinion
E minor By far the easiest chord to learn
A Major Also easy
A Minor This is my favorite. Not super hard to learn.
C Major Super hard. Almost impossible. Until you realise that it is kinda the same chord as A Minor.
D Minor Easy if you have no fingers
B Minor Easy if you have a lot of fingers
The chords of your favorite song Depends. Either super easy or you might be at risk of hating this song solely for the fact that you can't play it ever
